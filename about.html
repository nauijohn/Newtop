About our company

  Newtop Corporation is comprised of 25 years of experience in the extremely competitive and highly specialized field of Logistics and Heavylift. Having founded only in 1997, but is fast growing and has started to make its mark in the cargo handling business, Ensuring prompt and efficient service, its roster of satisfied clients include: Yusen Air & Sea Service, Nippon Konpo Phils. Inc., ATE Freight, Amertron Inc. etc.

This corporation is actually the realization of the dream of a group experienced professionals who wanted to build a business of their own. The dream of these Filipinos was shared by MR. TAKESHI ISHIDA, the founding chairman who provided the important foreign connection and back up needed to start the ball rolling. Newtop ownership is 60% Filipino and 40% Japanese.
